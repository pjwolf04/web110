What is circuit racing?

Circuit racing is a type of racing where cars go around a designated track, mostly on paved roads, but some races can be on dirt and other surfaces.

What is a type of circuit racing series?

A type of circuit racing series is the Formula series.

What is Formula Series?

Formula series is an open-wheel racing series where the cars are regulated in each division.

Formula Series

  • F1

    This is where the top drivers in Formula compete with the fastest cars.

  • F2

    This is a secondary development series aiming to develop younger drivers.

  • F3

    This is a third division focusing on developing young drivers.

  • F4

    This is a fourth division where younger drivers participate.
