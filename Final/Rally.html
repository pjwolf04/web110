What is rally racing?

Rally racing is a type of racing where cars go around a designated track mostly on dirt and gravel, but some races can be on paved roads and other surfaces.

What is a type of rally racing series?

A type of rally racing series is the WRC.

What is WRC ?

The WRC is an rally racing series where the cars are regulated in each division.

WRC

  • WRC

    This is where the top drivers in Formula compete with the fastest cars.

  • WRC 2

    This is a secondary development series aiming to develop younger drivers.

  • WRC 3

    This is a third division focusing on developing young drivers.

  • WRC 4

    This is a fourth division where younger drivers participate.

Regional

An example of a Regional division would be ERC or European Rally Championship.

Nation

A example of a nation division in rally would be the FRC or Finnish Rally Championship.
